Getting Started

Valoria MMO is a massive multiplayer online role play game where thousand of players meets each others in the whole world of Valoria.

Discover unique areas to explore.

Hunt incredible and strong creatures.

Find mysterious chest among the world.

Meet other players.

Perform party with players to help you in your journey.

Train your abilities such as magic, distance, melee or shielding.

Dominate the rank list.

Build the best equipment.

Collect more than 100 unique items.

Valoria is on a earlier stage with a powerful engine behind and we are doing a lot of progress to become the best mmo ever.
